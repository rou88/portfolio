01 PUZZLE

PUZZLE represents a captivating public intervention and art installation featuring an oversized concrete puzzle, its fragments thoughtfully scattered across the streets of Gorizia and Nova Gorica. This immersive experience beckons the public to embark on a journey of discovery, encouraging them to seek, collect, and piece together the puzzle.

02 DREAMCATCHER

DREAMCATCHER is an immersive art installation, a captivating piece that serves as a visual metaphor for the juxtaposition of two virtual spaces, embodying a duality of existence—an exploration of contrasts, distinctions, and the myriad 'worlds' we inhabit. It encapsulates the realm we seek to depart from and the one we aspire to attain.

04 BORDER

BORDER is a poignant video installation that portrays the intersection of two distinct frontiers in my life: the Palestine - Jordan border, which I traverse when entering and leaving Palestine, and the Italy - Slovenia border, a part of my daily journey to school. This installation serves as a reimagined perspective on my experiences navigating these borders, symbolizing my ongoing exploration of border-crossing.

Academy of Applied Arts

Rijeka / Croatia, 2016

05 DONI

DONI is a captivating stop-motion animated music video created for the song of the same name by a local Slovenian band hailing from Nova Gorica. This artistic endeavor was a collaborative effort, realized with a talented group of students from the University of Nova Gorica in Slovenia. The video has received recognition and acclaim, being featured in numerous animation and film festivals across Slovenia, including the Animateka 2016 festival.

Animateka 2016

Ljubljana / Slovenia 2016

RAWAN HOURANI

Rawan Hourani has a Bachelor's degree in Interior Design from Petra University in Amman – Jordan and a Master's degree in Media Art and Practices (Contemporary Art) from the University of Nova Gorica – Slovenia.

A ceramist reflecting the harmony of functionality and aesthetics. Founder and Art Director of ROUWARE. Based in Athens.

Previously, I co-founded and directed The Space Berlin e.V., where I led artistic programming, media production, and cross-institutional collaborations. My work focused on curating inclusive spaces through exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events—bridging art, community, and dialogue.

I bring a multidisciplinary perspective rooted in spatial awareness, visual storytelling, and contemporary art.
