RAWAN HOURANI

Rawan Hourani is a designer, artist, and cultural producer working across digital experience, visual identity, and installation. Her work is grounded in narrative clarity and the poetics of form.

She holds a BA in Interior Design from Petra University in Amman and an MA in Media Arts and Practices from the University of Nova Gorica.

Previously, Rawan co-founded and led The Space Berlin, an arts and media hub for diaspora storytelling. She is currently based in Athens, where she runs the ceramics practice ROUWARE.
