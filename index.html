RAWAN HOURANI

Rawan Hourani has a Bachelor's degree in Interior Design from Petra University in Amman – Jordan and a Master's degree in Media Art and Practices (Contemporary Art) from the University of Nova Gorica – Slovenia.

A ceramist reflecting the harmony of functionality and aesthetics. Founder and Art Director of ROUWARE. Based in Athens.

Previously, I co-founded and directed The Space Berlin e.V., where I led artistic programming, media production, and cross-institutional collaborations. My work focused on curating inclusive spaces through exhibitions, workshops, and cultural events—bridging art, community, and dialogue.

I bring a multidisciplinary perspective rooted in spatial awareness, visual storytelling, and contemporary art.

01 PUZZLE

PUZZLE represents a captivating public intervention and art installation featuring an oversized concrete puzzle, its fragments thoughtfully scattered across the streets of Gorizia and Nova Gorica. This immersive experience beckons the public to embark on a journey of discovery, encouraging them to seek, collect, and piece together the puzzle.
